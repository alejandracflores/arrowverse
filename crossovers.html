CROSSORVERS IN THE ARROWVERSE

Here is a list of all the Arrwoverse crossovers:

  1. In the second season of Arrow we had the first appearance of Barry Allen, even without his powers. Episodes 2×08 and 2×09 introduced this forensic scientist who was going to Starling City to investigate a case.

  2. In 2×19 Arrow, Cisco Ramon and Caitlin Snow were introduced as simple scientists from S.T.A.R. Laboratories.

  3. In 1×01 of The Flash , Barry and Oliver had a conversation on a rooftop where the archer explained the path of a hero.

  4. In the 1×04 episode of The Flash, Felicity (Arrow's character) appears in Central City to help the Flash Team.

  5. CROSSOVER: FLASH VS ARROW

  6. In The Flash 1×18. Felicity and Ray Palmer travel back to Central City. In this episode Flash and Atom will team up to stop Brie Larvan and his robot bee army.

  7. In the 1×19 episode of The Flash, Cisco and Joe West travel to Starling City to investigate the secret of Harrison Wells. Quentin Lance and Laurel will appear and Cisco will create in this episode the device that will give Black Canary the power to scream and stun their enemies.

  8. In The Flash, chapter 1×22, Oliver meets with Barry to help him catch the Reverse of Flash.

  9. In the 3×23 episode of Arrow, Flash has a brief but crucial appearance to save the day.

  10. MEGA CROSSOVER: HEROES JOIN FORCES

  11. In the 1×01 DC's Legends of Tomorrow episode, right in the pilot of this series, Laurel and Oliver will make a small appearance.

  12. In 2×15 The Flash, the Flash Team takes on King Shark. The government agency A.R.G.U.S. appears, Lyla Michaels and John Diggle will help stop him. In this episode Cisco will upgrade the Spartan suit for Diggle.

  13. Legends of Tomorrow, in 1×06, the legends go to the year 2046 and a Star City is found in the ruins. In this chapter we see an old man Oliver Queen.

  14. In the 4×19 of Arrow we see Barry Allen with Oliver facing the death of an important character.

  15. CROSSOVER: WORLD’S FINEST

  16. The 2×22 episode of The Flash first introduces Black Siren, a villain from Earth 2, a Laurel Lance double, who will later have better character development in Arrow until her redemption.

  17. In The Flash 2×02 Barry will go to Felicity for advice.

  18. MEGA CROSSOVER: INVASION!

  19. During the second season of Legends of Tomorrow, we are introduced to the 3 main villains. Malcolm Merlyn (who appeared in Arrow even as a villain several seasons), Damien Darhk (main villain of the fourth season of the emerald archer) and the Reverse Flash (villain of Flash) will form the Legion of Evil. During this season we will also see Black Flash, a character we met in the second season of The Flash.

  20. In the 3×11 of The Flash, Vibe and Gypsy have a fight between dimensions and for a few seconds they cross a gap and appear in CATCO's offices, where Eve Teschmacher, Lex Luthor's assistant, appears.

  21. Arrow, in the 5×11, Green Arrow asks Singh, the Central City police captain, for help and so that he can confirm that the person on the phone is not a prankster, Barry Allen leaves him a super-speed note confirming that it is the archer who is behind the phone.

  22. CROSSOVER DUET

  23. The Flash, in the 3×22 Barry will have to go to A.R.G.U.S. to ask Lyla for help. Captain Cold also returns during this episode.

  24. In the 2×16 of Legends of Tomorrow, the Legion of Evil creates a new reality in which Felicity appears as a vigilante. The next chapter, 17, features Laurel Lance of Earth 1 as a hallucination/guide for Sara.

  25. Arrow, the 5×23, features Malcolm Merlyn and Captain Boomerang again.

  26. In Legends of Tomorrow with its third season, Wally West makes presence in several episodes, until joining the team temporarily only during this season. Damien Darhk will also appear again, a baby Dominator in a tribute episode to E.T. (3×04) and a brief scene in the 3×05 where some legends will ask for help to Curtis, character of Arrow.

  27. In the 4×05 of The Flash, Iris West performs a bachelorette party. Felicity Smoak attends the party.

  28. MEGA CROSSOVER: CRISIS ON EARTH-X

  29. During the third season of Legends of Tomorrow we have several chapters with Constantine in the middle, this character was introduced in the fourth season of Arrow and currently has two seasons in Legends.

  30. In the 6×13 of Arrow, Flash has its moment. Oliver asks Barry to take him and Diggle at super speed along with the villain to get there as soon as possible.

  31. In the 4×19 of The Flash, a Nazi version of Laurel Lance appears, which slips through a portal to Earth 1 when the Flash Team travels to Earth X to look for Leo Snart, one of the Freedom Fighters introduced in the previous mega crossover.

  32. In the 6×23 Arrow, Sara has a brief cameo.

  33. The 5×08 episode of The Flash is the 100th in the series. So they pay homage to the whole series. We will see several events and characters from the past.

  34. MEGA CROSSOVER: ELSEWORLDS

  35. The 150th episode of Arrow's series is the 5×12. This time they make a special, which as with the other anniversaries, pays homage to the series. It is a documentary episode without leaving the main plot where we see again old characters, even Barry Allen appears in an interview talking about Oliver.

  36. In the chapter 7×18 of Arrow, (Lost Canary), Sara returns to help the Arrow Team.

  37. In Supergirl's 4×22, the Monitor appears again bringing J'onn J'onnz's brother to Earth 38. And taking Lex Luthor's dying body with him.

  38. The Monitor also appears in the final chapter of Arrow's seventh season to remove Oliver from his family and enforce his deal in Elseworlds.

  39. MEGA CROSSOVER: CRISIS ON INFINITE EARTHS

  40. In the 5×10 of The Flash appears John Diggle.
